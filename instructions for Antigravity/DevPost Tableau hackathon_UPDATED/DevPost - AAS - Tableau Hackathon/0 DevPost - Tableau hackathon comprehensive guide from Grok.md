2. Eligibility
Who Can Participate

Individuals: Eligible Individuals who are (i) 18 years of age or older (20+ in Japan) or who have reached the age of majority in their jurisdiction of residence as of the date of entry, and (ii) legal residents of the Eligibility Area.
Teams: Eligible Teams comprised of up to five (5) Eligible Individuals.
Organizations: Eligible Teams among employees within organizations (including corporations, not-for-profit corporations and other nonprofit organizations, limited liability companies, partnerships, and other legal entities) that exist and have been organized or incorporated at the time of entry and are registered in the Eligibility Area.
Additional Notes:
An Eligible Individual may join more than one Eligible Team and may also enter individually.
Each Eligible Individual and Eligible Team must submit a unique Eligible Submission (no duplicates).
For teams, appoint one Representative to act on behalf of the team.

Eligibility Area (Countries/Territories): Argentina, Australia, Belgium, Canada (excluding Quebec), France, Germany, India, Ireland, Japan, Luxembourg, Netherlands, New Zealand, South Africa, Spain, United Kingdom, United States of America (including District of Columbia).

Who Cannot Participate

Employees, contractors, consultants, officers, directors, and agents of Sponsor, its parent, divisions, subsidiaries, agents, promotional partners, advertising/promotion agencies, and prize suppliers (Hackathon Entities) – or those who were such within 2 years prior to start.
Immediate family (spouse, parents, siblings, children) or household members of the above.
Current employees of entities in which Sponsor has invested and holds ownership.
Current employees of third-party sponsors.
Any Judge (or their employer).
Anyone whose participation creates a real or apparent conflict of interest (Sponsor's discretion).
Employees or officials of any government entity.
Void: Outside Eligibility Area or where prohibited/restricted by law. Subject to applicable laws.

General

Failure to meet requirements invalidates entry and forfeits prizes.
Sponsor may disqualify entrants that could reflect negatively on Sponsor's image.

3. How to Enter
Steps to Enter

Visit tableau2025.devpost.com and click “Join Hackathon” to register (create free Devpost account or log in).
Obtain access to environments/tools:
Tableau Next: Request Salesforce org access via form (updated from initial link; confirm via resources).
Tableau Cloud: Access via Tableau Developer Platform.

Create a video demonstrating your Project (see requirements below).
Complete “Enter a Submission” page during Submission Period.

Project Requirements

What to Create: Develop a new solution using Tableau Next and/or Tableau Cloud to solve a business problem.
Functionality: Must install/run consistently on intended platform and function as shown in video/description.
Platforms:
Tableau Next: Intended for and runs on Salesforce platform.
Tableau Cloud: Use Tableau Developer Platform and supported Tableau tools/APIs only.

New Apps Only: Created after Submission Period start.
Third-Party Integrations: Must be authorized.
Testing: Make available free-of-charge for Sponsor/Judges until end of Judging Period. If proprietary hardware, provide physical access upon request.
Financial Support Ban: No development with Sponsor's financial/preferential support prior to end of Submission Period.

Submission Requirements
Submissions must:

Include Project built with required environments/tools (Tableau Next: Use only provisioned org).
Text description explaining features/functionality.
Demonstration video:
<5 minutes (judges not required to watch beyond).
Shows Project functioning on intended device.
Uploaded publicly to YouTube, Vimeo, Facebook Video, or Youku; link provided.
No third-party trademarks, copyrighted music/material without permission.

Provide admin credentials to provisioned org (Tableau Next) for judging/testing.
Include Org ID for provisioned org (Tableau Next only).
1-2 sentence pitch.
Original work, solely owned by Entrants, no IP violations.
Multiple Entries: Allowed if unique/substantially different (Sponsor/Devpost discretion).
URL to code repository (e.g., GitHub); if private, grant access to @kglover29 and testing@devpost.com.
List of APIs/dev tools used + potential improvements.

Language Requirements

All materials in English, or provide English translations for video, description, instructions.

Team Representation

Appoint Representative (must be eligible); they handle decisions, including prizes.

Intellectual Property

Submission must be original, solely owned, no violations of IP/privacy rights.
May use third-party assistance if Entrant owns result.
Open source OK if licenses complied with and enhances functionality.
No confidential/proprietary/sensitive info; no harmful/illegal content; no malware.
Sponsor may disqualify at discretion.

Modifications

Drafts: Save drafts pre-submission; no changes post-Submission Period.
Post-Period: Limited mods allowed for IP/inappropriate content removal (must remain substantively same).
